# Honolulu ADU Review Skill
## Purpose
This skill provides the regulatory knowledge base for evaluating Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) plans against the City and County of Honolulu Department of Planning and Permitting (DPP) regulations, as well as State of Hawaii laws (e.g., SB3202).
## Scope
- Evaluates architectural plans for ADU compliance in Honolulu, Hawaii.
- Verifies zoning, setbacks, height limits, and parking requirements.
- Checks compliance with the Board of Water Supply (BWS) and Honolulu Fire Department (HFD).
