# Board of Water Supply (BWS) ADU Rules
## Capacity Verification (Pre-Form)
- Before a permit is issued, the owner must submit an 'Ohana/ADU Pre-Form to verify utility capacity.
- The BWS must verify that the water system has adequate supplies to assume the new ADU.
## Water Meters
- An additional water meter for the ADU is **optional**.
- Existing Meter: If the existing water meter size is sufficient for the property's new total demand, it can be used.
