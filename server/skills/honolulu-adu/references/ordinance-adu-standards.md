# Honolulu ADU Development Standards (ROH Chapter 21)
## Size Limits (Updated by Ordinance 25-2)
- **Lots 0 to 4,999 sq ft:** Maximum ADU size is 500 sq ft.
- **Lots 5,000 sq ft and larger:** Maximum ADU size is 1,000 sq ft.
## Lot Coverage and Placement
- The ADU must adhere to the underlying zoning district's maximum lot coverage (typically 50% for R-5).
- Impervious surface area of the lot must not exceed 75% of the zoning lot area.
- Can be attached or detached from the primary dwelling.
## Bathrooms and Kitchens
- Typically limited to up to 3 bathrooms per dwelling unit.
- Only one kitchen allowed per dwelling unit (ADU is its own dwelling unit).
