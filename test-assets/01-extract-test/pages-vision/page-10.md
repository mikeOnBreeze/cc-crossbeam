### Plan Notes - Foundation:

101. All foundation depths are into undisturbed soil.
102. Foundation bolts to be 5/8" with 7" embedment at 48" max, 12" max. dist. to all ends and sills. See detail 2/S2.
103. Type V cement with 2500 PSI concrete with water-cement ratio not to exceed 0.45. No special inspection required.
104. Use min. 7"x7"x3/2" washers.
105. Foundation sill plate to be preservative-treated wood.
106. Fasteners in contact with preservative-treated wood shall be hot dipped zinc-coated, galvanized stainless steel, silicon, bronze, or copper.
107. All (N) walls shall be connected to adjacent (E) concrete with drill & epoxy dowels (3/4" diameter) with Simpson Set 3C (ESR#4057). All such dowels shall have 8" min detail 7/S2.
