## Chapter 4 — Residential Mandatory Measures (Left Column, Continuing Down)

### Section 4.102 Definitions

Terms defined in Chapter 2 and as adopted here for reference.

- **HARDSCAPE:** Non-permeable surface material applied to soil, grass, vegetation or base in exterior areas.
- **MOTTLES:** Stains due to mineral oxidation in soil. Stains after construction of natural grade materials.

### 4.106 SITE DEVELOPMENT

**4.106.2 STORM WATER DRAINAGE AND RETENTION DURING CONSTRUCTION.** Projects which disturb less than one acre of soil and are not part of a larger plan of development that would disturb one or more acres shall manage storm water drainage during construction. Best management practices shall be utilized to prevent discharge of sediment and other pollutants.

**4.106.3 GRADING AND PAVING.** Construction plans shall comply with drainage system and run-off requirements. Grading plans shall be designed to manage stormwater and prevent erosion.

Items to be shown on plans include:
1. Drainage
2. Stormwater controls
3. Erosion and Sediment controls
4. Grading and Soil systems
5. Landscaping

**4.106.4 Electric vehicle (EV) charging for new construction.** New construction shall comply with Electric Vehicle charging requirements per current code.
