## 2022 Single-Family Residential Mandatory Requirements Summary — Column 1 (Left Third)

### Building Envelope Section
1. **Air Leakage:** Building envelope air sealing requirements. Caulking, sealing, and weather-stripping per applicable code sections. Joints, penetrations, and openings in building envelope shall be sealed.
2. **Ceiling/Attic Insulation:** Requirements for ceiling insulation installation, including R-value specifications and installation methods.
3. **Cool Roof:** Cool roof requirements for low-slope and steep-slope roofing, including aged solar reflectance and thermal emittance values.
4. **Doors:** Door specifications including U-factor requirements for opaque and glazed doors.
5. **Insulation Certification by Manufacturer:** Insulation must be certified by the Department of Consumer Affairs, Bureau of Household Goods and Services.
6. **Insulation — General Requirements:** Installation requirements including proper fit, continuous coverage, and vapor retarder specifications.
7. **Metal Buildings:** Metal building insulation requirements (if applicable).
8. **Roof Deck, Ceiling and Rafter Specifications:** Requirements for insulation between rafters and above roof deck, including R-values and installation specifications.

### Additional Building Envelope Items
9. **Roofing Products:** Roofing product specifications and aged solar reflectance requirements.
10. **Steeper-Sloped Roofs:** Requirements specific to steep-slope roofing materials.
11. **Walls:** Wall insulation requirements including cavity and continuous insulation R-values.
12. **Vapor Retarder:** Vapor retarder requirements for climate zone compliance.

### Fenestration Section
13. **Windows and Glazing:** U-factor and SHGC requirements for fenestration products.
14. **Exterior Doors:** Specifications for exterior door thermal performance.
15. **Skylights:** Skylight U-factor and SHGC requirements.

18. **Raised Floor:** Raised floor insulation specifications.

## 2022 Single-Family Residential Mandatory Requirements Summary — Column 2 (Center-Left Third)

### Mechanical Systems Section
19. **Pilot Lights:** Requirements regarding standing pilot lights — generally prohibited for gas heating equipment.
20. **Setback Thermostat:** Programmable setback thermostat requirements for HVAC systems.
21. **Heating and Cooling Units:** Equipment efficiency requirements and sizing specifications.
22. **Gravity Venting Appliances:** Requirements for proper venting of gravity-vent appliances.
23. **Duct Sealing:** All HVAC duct joints and seams shall be sealed with mastic, tape, or aerosol sealant. Duct leakage testing requirements.
24. **Duct Insulation:** Minimum duct insulation R-values for supply and return ducts.

### Ventilation Section
25. **Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) and Mechanical Ventilation:** Whole-house mechanical ventilation requirements per ASHRAE 62.2. Required ventilation rate based on floor area and number of bedrooms.
26. **Kitchen Ventilation:** Range hood requirements — minimum airflow rates, sound ratings.
27. **Bathroom Ventilation:** Bathroom exhaust fan requirements — minimum airflow rates, ENERGY STAR rating.

### Water Heating Section
28. **Water Heating Systems:** Water heater efficiency requirements and installation specifications.
29. **Pipe Insulation:** Hot water pipe insulation requirements — R-value specifications.
30. **Solar Water Heating:** Solar water heating readiness requirements (if applicable).

### Plumbing Section
31. **Plumbing Fixtures:** Water efficiency requirements for plumbing fixtures and fittings.
