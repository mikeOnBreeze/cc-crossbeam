## Chapter 4 — Residential Mandatory Measures (Left Column, Main Body)

### Section 4.102 Definitions
- References materials defined in Chapter 2 and are included here for reference.
- **ADHESIVE**: An adhesive is any substance that is used to bond surfaces. Lists types.
- **SEALANT**: Any material used to seal joints.

### 4.106 Site Development
- Discussion of reduction in use of potable resources and stormwater runoff.

### 4.106.1 Storm Water Drainage and Retention During Construction
- Projects which disturb less than one acre of soil must manage stormwater according to local requirements.
- **SWPPP** or erosion control plan required.

### 4.106.3 Grading and Paving
- Construction plan shall indicate how stormwater is managed, retained on site, or drained to appropriate locations.
- Requirements for stormwater management:
  1. Grading plans
  2. Drainage plans and disposal systems
  3. Landscape/irrigation
  4. Vegetated swales
  5. Rain gardens
  6. Cisterns and rain barrels

### 4.106.4 Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging for New Construction
- New construction shall comply with EV charging provisions.
- **4.106.4.1**: Electric vehicle supply equipment (EVSE) required for single family and townhomes.
- References to EV-ready requirements per relevant code sections.

### 4.106.4.1 Parking Spaces
- Referenced parking space requirements for EV charging installations.

## Division 4.3 — Water Efficiency and Conservation (Center)

### 4.303 Indoor Water Use
- **4.303.1 Kitchen faucets**: Max flow rate provisions.
- **4.303.1.1**: Kitchen faucets shall not exceed specified flow rates.
- **4.303.1.2**: Optional lower-flow kitchen faucets.
- **4.303.1.3**: Lavatory faucets shall not exceed specified GPM.
- **4.303.1.4**: Showerheads shall not exceed specified GPM flow rate.
- **4.303.2 Water closets and urinals**: Max flush volume provisions.
  - **4.303.2.1**: Water closets (toilets) effective flush volume shall not exceed 1.28 GPF.
  - **4.303.2.2**: Urinals shall not exceed 0.5 GPF.
