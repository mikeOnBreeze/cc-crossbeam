## 2022 Single Family Residential Mandatory Requirements Summary (Top Section — 3 Columns)

The top half of the sheet contains a dense, multi-column summary of mandatory requirements from the 2022 Title 24 Building Energy Efficiency Standards. The content is organized into numbered sections with paragraph-form descriptions.

### Column 1 (Top-Left)

#### Building Envelope Requirements

1. **Air Leakage:** Building envelope sealing requirements. Joints, penetrations, and cracks in the building envelope shall be caulked, gasketed, weatherstripped, or otherwise sealed to limit air leakage.

2. **Ceiling/Attic:** Insulation requirements for ceiling and attic assemblies. R-value requirements for ceiling insulation.

3. **Insulation Certification by Manufacturer:** Insulation shall be certified by the Department of Consumer Affairs, Bureau of Household Goods and Services.

4. **Walls:** Wall insulation requirements including R-value specifications for framing cavities and continuous insulation.

5. **Raised Floor:** Insulation requirements for raised floors over ventilated crawl spaces.

6. **Slab Edge:** Slab-edge insulation requirements where applicable.

7. **Fenestration Products:** Window and glazing product requirements including certification, labeling, and default U-factor/SHGC values.

8. **Roof Deck, Ceiling and Rafter Roof Construction:** Requirements for cool roof surfaces, radiant barriers, or above-deck insulation. Roof deck insulation specifications.

9. **Below Grade Walls:** Insulation requirements for below-grade wall assemblies.

10. **Fireplaces:** Requirements for fireplace dampers, combustion air, and gas log lighters. Decorative gas appliance requirements.

### Column 2 (Top-Center)

#### Mechanical / HVAC Requirements

11. **Pipe Insulation:** Hot water pipe insulation requirements. Minimum R-value for pipe insulation based on pipe diameter.

12. **Space Heating and Cooling:** Equipment efficiency requirements. Load calculations per ACCA Manual J or equivalent.

13. **Space Cooling and Heating Loads:** Requirements for heating and cooling load calculations.

14. **Duct Insulation:** Minimum R-value for supply and return ducts. Location-based requirements (conditioned vs unconditioned space).

15. **Duct Sealing:** All ducts shall be sealed with mastic or listed duct tape. SMACNA standards for duct construction and sealing.

16. **Ventilation:** Mechanical ventilation requirements per ASHRAE 62.2. Exhaust fan requirements for kitchens and bathrooms.

17. **Refrigerant Piping:** Insulation requirements for refrigerant suction lines.

18. **Snow Melt and Freeze Prevention Systems:** Requirements for snow melt systems (generally not applicable in Climate Zone 8).

### Column 3 (Top-Right)

#### Water Heating, Lighting, and Additional Requirements

19. **Space Conditioning System Action Rates and Efficiency:** Equipment efficiency standards and minimum efficiency requirements for HVAC systems.

20. **Water Heating:** Water heater efficiency requirements. Pipe insulation for hot water distribution. Recirculation system controls.

21. **Pool and Spa Systems:** Equipment requirements for pool and spa heating, pumps, and covers.

22. **Lighting and Electrical:** High-efficacy lighting requirements. All permanently installed luminaires shall be high efficacy. Controls and dimming requirements.

23. **Appliances:** Requirements for appliance energy efficiency.

## 2022 Single Family Residential Mandatory Requirements Summary (Bottom Section — 3 Columns)

The bottom half continues the mandatory requirements summary.

### Column 1 (Bottom-Left)

#### Detailed Provisions

- **Ceramic Tile:** Requirements for adhesive and grouting materials.
- **Comfort Provisions:** Indoor temperature control requirements.
- **Air Conditioning:** Specifications for air conditioning system installation, testing, and verification.
- **Solar Ready:** Solar zone requirements for new construction. Conduit and panel requirements for future PV installation.
- **Combustion Appliances:** Requirements for sealed combustion or direct-vent appliances. Atmospheric venting restrictions.
- **Dwelling Ventilation:** Whole-house mechanical ventilation requirements per ASHRAE 62.2. Local exhaust ventilation for kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Cooling Ventilation:** Whole-house fan or central fan integrated ventilation options.
- **Comfort Zone:** Thermostat requirements and setpoint specifications.

### Column 2 (Bottom-Center)

#### Verification and Testing

- **HERS Verification Requirements:** Items requiring HERS verification including duct leakage testing, refrigerant charge verification, airflow verification, and fan watt draw.
- **Installation Certificates:** Required documentation for equipment installation verification.
- **Quality Insulation Installation (QII):** Requirements for insulation installation verification by HERS rater.
- **Fenestration Verification:** Window/door installation verification requirements.
- **Duct Testing:** Duct leakage test requirements and thresholds.
- **Electrical Power Distribution:** Requirements for electrical system efficiency.
- **Demand Response for Single or Multiple Dwelling Units:** Requirements for demand response capable thermostats and appliances.

### Column 3 (Bottom-Right)

#### Additional Mandatory Measures

- **Energy Storage Readiness:** Provisions for future battery energy storage system installation.
- **Photovoltaic Requirements:** PV system sizing requirements for new construction. Minimum system size based on conditioned floor area and climate zone.
- **Battery Storage:** Requirements for battery-ready infrastructure.
- **Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging:** EV charging readiness requirements. Dedicated circuit provisions.
- **Plumbing:** Hot water distribution system requirements. Pipe insulation specifications.
- **Moisture Control:** Vapor barrier and moisture management requirements.
