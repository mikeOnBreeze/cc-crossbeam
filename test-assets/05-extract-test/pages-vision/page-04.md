## DIVISION 4.5 — ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY (continued) (Center)

### 4.505 Indoor Air Quality and Exhaust (Top-Right)
- HVAC systems: must comply with mandatory requirements and test
- Must be ENERGY STAR compliant and be ducted to terminate outside, not into attic
- Bathroom exhaust fans: ENERGY STAR rated
- Identify conditions that require additional equipment or measures (e.g., proximity to high-volume roadway)

### 4.507 Environmental Comfort
- Heat island mitigation: roof shall meet requirements for aged solar reflectance and thermal emittance
- Cool roof requirements per Title 24

### 4.504.1 Carpet Systems
- All carpet systems installed in building interior shall comply with VOC requirements per testing programs

### 4.504.4 Resilient Flooring Systems
- Mineral-content flooring materials with specified requirements
- References: https://www.cdph.ca.gov/Programs/CCDPHP/DEODC/EHLB/IAQ/Pages/VOC.aspx

### 4.504.5 Composite Wood Products
- Hardwood plywood, particleboard, and medium density fiberboard: comply with CARB ATCM formaldehyde limits

### 4.505 Interior Moisture Control
- Per ASHRAE 160 or equivalents for moisture vapor

### 4.506.1 Concrete Slab Foundations
- Concrete in direct contact with earth: provide vapor retarder per CBC Section 1907.1

## Section 4.506 — Moisture Content of Building Materials (Center-Right)
