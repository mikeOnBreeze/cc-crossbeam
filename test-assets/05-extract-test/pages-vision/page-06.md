## Plumbing Fixture Notes (Center-Right)
- All plumbing fixtures and fittings shall comply with 2019 CPC Section 402
- Maximum flow rates:
  - Lavatory faucets: 1.2 GPM max at 60 PSI
  - Kitchen faucets: 1.8 GPM max
  - Showerheads: a maximum flow rate of 1.8 gallons per minute at 80 PSI
  - Water closets: max 1.28 GPF (HET)
- All residential and commercial properties: water-conserving plumbing fixtures required

## Smoke Alarm Notes (Center-Left)
1. In each sleeping room
2. Outside each sleeping area in the immediate vicinity of the bedrooms
3. On each story of the dwelling, not including crawl spaces and attics
4. Smoke alarms shall be interconnected
5. Within a room or opening outside of a bedroom that contains bedroom doorway — 21 ft radius (all units a-c)

## Carbon Monoxide Alarm Notes (Center-Left)
- Interconnected hard-wired carbon monoxide alarms with battery backup shall be placed in the following locations:
  1. Outside of each separate sleeping area in the immediate vicinity
  2. In each bedroom
  3. Every level including basement; shall be installed in accordance with NFPA 72

## Shower / Bath Notes (Center-Left)
- Shower compartments with shower heads shall be provided with a smooth, non-absorbent surface to a height of not less than 70"
- Cement fiber board (not gypsum); glass may be utilized in non-hazardous splash zones shall be used at base
- Min. tub: 30 x 60 minimum width and max spa shall provide at shower entry 24"
- Shower receptor: non-slip surface

## Clothes Dryer Exhaust Ducting (Center)
- Length limited to 14' with 2 elbows, deduct lengths on sizes as permitted or required
- Dryer exhaust: comply with CMC 504 for modifications (max length 14 ft)
