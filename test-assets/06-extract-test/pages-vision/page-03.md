### Section 301 General
- **301.1 SCOPE:** Applies to newly constructed low-rise residential buildings, hotels, motels, and lodging houses

## Division 4.1 — Planning and Design (Left Column, Middle-to-Bottom)

### Section 4.106 Site Development
- **4.106.2:** Storm water pollution prevention
  - Storm water management practices accomplished through site design and treatment strategies
  - Projects that add or replace more than 2,500 sq ft of impervious surface
  - Onsite retention of the 85th percentile 24-hour storm event
  - Reference to State Water Resources Control Board for projects disturbing one acre or more

- **4.106.3 GRADING AND PAVING:** Construction grading shall minimize site grading to drainage system, landscaping with climate-appropriate plants

### 4.106.4 Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging
- **4.106.4.1:** EV charging for new construction — comply with Section 4.106.4.1.1
- **4.106.4.1.1:** Electric vehicle charging stations (EVCS)
  - Low-rise multifamily: EVCS at 10%
  - Single space: one ready space shall be installed
  - A NEMA 14-50 receptacle
  - If 2+ EV spaces: at least one Level 2 EVSE

- **4.106.4.1.2:** EVCS for low-rise multifamily buildings
  - One Level 2 EV charging installation or Level 2 EV-capable
  - Raceway from electrical service

- **4.106.4.1.3:** EVCS spaces and EV chargers standard dimensions
  - Minimum EVCS space length: 17 feet (5182 mm)
  - Accessible EV spaces requirements

- **4.106.4.2:** Parking spaces
  - 10% of total parking spaces shall be equipped
  - When one Level 2 EV charging installed, install at least one Level 2 EV-capable

## Section 4.106.4.2.1 (Center Column, Upper)
- EV charging stations for new one-family and two-family dwellings and townhomes with attached private garages
  - One Level 2 EV charging installation or Level 2 EV-ready
  - Raceway, wiring, EVSE
