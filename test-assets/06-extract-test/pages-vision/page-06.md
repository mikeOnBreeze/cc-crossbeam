## Smoke Alarm Notes (Center-Left)
- Smoke alarms with battery backup shall be placed in the following locations:
  1. In each sleeping room
  2. Outside of each sleeping area in the immediate vicinity of the bedrooms
  3. On each story of the dwelling, but not including crawl spaces and uninhabitable attic spaces
  4. Not over 12" from a door or opening outside of a bathroom that contains bathtub or shower (NFPA 72 4-5.1.1)
- Alarms shall be interconnected

## Carbon Monoxide Alarm Notes (Center)
- CO alarms shall be placed in the following locations:
  1. Outside of each sleeping area in the immediate vicinity of the bedroom(s)
  2. On every level of a dwelling unit
- CO alarms listed and labeled, installed with battery backup and shall be installed in accordance with the manufacturer's instructions
- Required in all spaces that have fuel-burning appliances

## Shower / Bath Notes (Center-Left)
1. Shower receptors and tub areas shall be finished with cement, glass, plastic, or non-absorbent materials
2. Apply not less than 6" above floor surface (R307.2)
3. Tub walls to 70" and shower areas and wall and ceiling panels in shower areas required
4. All fixtures shall have approved backflow prevention
5. As of January 1, 2014, do not require that noncompliant plumbing fixtures in residential and commercial properties built on or before January 1, 1994 be replaced with water-conserving plumbing fixtures when the property is undergoing alteration or improvement
